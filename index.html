HOW TO PLAY
OBJECTIVE

Earn US dollars by buying and selling Bitcoin. Hit each cash balance goal to unlock a cheaper buy cost and a higher next goal.

BUYING BITCOIN

Each buy costs a flat dollar amount (starting at $200). The amount of BTC you receive depends on the current price — but you cannot see the exact price. Use the price signal chart to make your call.

THE PRICE SIGNAL

The chart shows the last 60 ticks of BTC price movement with no numbers on the Y axis. You cannot see whether price is at $30k or $80k — only the shape of recent movement. Read it for:

  • Slope — is the line trending up or down lately?
  • Volatility — are the peaks and valleys tight or wild?
  • Momentum — did it spike recently, or has it been flat?
SELLING BITCOIN

Each sell offloads exactly 20% of your current BTC stack and returns US dollars at the current (hidden) price. Press it multiple times to scale out, or 5 times to fully exit.

GOALS & UPGRADES

Goals track your top high USD cash balance. Sell your BTC to push it up. Hit a goal and your buy cost drops by $2.

HACKERS

Hackers attack on a timer, starting every 30 seconds. When the countdown hits zero, they steal 10% of your BTC. You can't stop them — only slow them down.

  • Pay $50 to upgrade your firewall, adding +5 seconds to the hacker interval
  • Each upgrade stacks — buy multiple times to push the timer further out
  • The countdown resets after each attack
  • If you have no BTC, the attack does nothing — but the timer keeps ticking
